FAQ

Your questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about getting started with PresenceIQ.

No app download is required — ever. Members simply open their phone's native camera, point it at the QR code, and tap the link that appears. It works on any modern iPhone or Android device without installing anything.
You can display the QR code on any screen — a laptop, projector, TV, or tablet works perfectly. You can also print it and stick it on a door, table, or noticeboard. Each session generates a unique QR code that you can access from your admin dashboard.
PresenceIQ uses time-limited QR codes that expire after the session ends, and optionally a location-lock feature that only accepts check-ins within a set radius. This means the code only works for the duration and location of your session, preventing remote or fraudulent check-ins.
Admins can manually mark attendance in the dashboard in seconds for members who don't have a smartphone. The manual entry is time-stamped and included in all reports exactly like a QR check-in, so your records stay complete.
Yes. PresenceIQ is built for UK organisations with GDPR compliance as a core principle. Your data is hosted on UK servers, encrypted at rest and in transit, and never sold or shared with third parties. You remain the data controller at all times, and members can request their data deleted.
Absolutely. You can create as many programmes as you need — for example, Sunday Service, Youth Group, and Bible Study can all exist under one church account, each with its own member list, sessions, and reports. Starter plan and above support multiple programmes.
After each session ends, PresenceIQ automatically sends a personalised confirmation email to everyone who attended, and a caring follow-up message to those who were absent. You can customise the email templates with your own wording, logo, and tone. SMS follow-ups are available on Growth and Pro plans.
Yes — our Free plan is free forever with no credit card required. All paid plans also include a 14-day free trial so you can experience the full features before committing. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time with no lock-in contracts.
