How it works

Three steps. Seconds flat.

From setup to your first attendance report — no technical knowledge required.

01

Apply or join

Organisations apply at app.presenceiq.app. Members register through a join link, membership QR, or session check-in page from their organisation.

02

Admin approval

Organisation applications are reviewed. Member registrations are approved by your admin before the first check-in.

03

Scan, tap, follow up

Members check in in seconds. Admins get attendance records, messaging templates, and optional follow-ups after each session.
